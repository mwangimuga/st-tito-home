About the School

St. Tito High School is a girls boarding school established in 1997. In barely a decade, the school has expanded in leaps and bounds it has considerably displayed excellent performance in academics, discipline co-curricular activities and the day to day social life of the students. The school is centrally placed along Wangige - Ndenderu road, at Karura Shopping Centre. From Nairobi, it is only 15kms or 15 minutes drive from the city centre. Matatus are boarded next to the fire station, Tom Mboya Street No. 108, Nairobi. The school is situated in a 10 acre land which poses a serene undulating landscape, catalyzing the environment for academic excellence and an adequate play ground
